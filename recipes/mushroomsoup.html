Creamy Mushroom Soup Recipe

Description

Creamy mushroom soup using canned pantry staples.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz canned mushrooms (drained)
  • 10.5 oz Cream of Mushroom canned soup (I use Campbell's)
  • 1/2 yellow onion (Julienne cut)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 6 oz canned evaporated milk
  • 2 tsp heavy cream
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • seasoning salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Julienne cut or dice half a yellow onion and drain canned mushrooms.
  2. Add mushrooms, onion, and butter to saucepan and saute at medium-high heat until onion starts to brown a little. If onion begins to stick to the pan beforehand, add a little bit of water.
  3. Add the canned soup, evaporated milk, and water to the pan and stir.
  4. Now add the heavy cream and coconut oil (or oil of choice) and stir to combine once again.
  5. Add seasoning salt (or plain salt) and pepper to taste.
  6. Bring soup to a boil, then stir and reduce heat.
  7. Simmer soup at a medium-low heat for 2 more minutes then serve.
