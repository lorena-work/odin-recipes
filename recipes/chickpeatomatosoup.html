Creamy Chickpea-Tomato Soup Recipe

Description

Creamy and flavorful chickpea and tomato soup using canned pantry staples.

Ingredients

  • 14.5 oz canned diced tomato (I use Hunts Basil, Garlic & Oregano)
  • 16 oz canned tomato sauce (I use two 8 oz Hunts cans)
  • 2 tsp heavy cream
  • 1 cup milk of choice
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Seasoning salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 16 oz canned chickpeas (drained)
  • 1 cup spinach

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a pot and stir to combine. Make sure to drain the liquid from the canned chickpeas before adding them to the pot.
  2. Cover with a lid and set stove to medium-high heat. Remove lid to stir every minute or so until the soup is boiling.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and remove the lid. Taste and adjust seasoning salt if needed.
  4. Serve soup and optionally enjoy with toast or grilled cheese on the side for a full hearty meal.
